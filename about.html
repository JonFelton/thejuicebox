ABOUT US

Who We Are

We're just a group of folks who want to enlighten and enrich the lives of others through the exploration of music forms that originated from black expression. We desire this site to be viewed as a celebration of black history and a tribute to some of the musical innovations from these genres that still influence music today.

If you have any questions concerning our content or if you'd like to make a comment or suggestion on areas we could improve, just shoot us an email and we'll try to respond swiftly.
