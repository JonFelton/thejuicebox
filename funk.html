What's the Juicebox?

The Juicebox is a place where we explore the history and sound of music styles that originated from the heart and soul of Black America. We jump into Hip Hop, take a deeper look at Jazz, have a dance with Funk, and marinate on the Blues. We want to appreciate and better understand the past and present of these generes and hopefully discover how they have made an impact on modern music today. Most of all, we just want to enjoy the music.
